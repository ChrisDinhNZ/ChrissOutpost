About

This blog is a space for me to capture and share some of the things I learnt along the way as a software developer. While the core function of this blog is documenting software and technology topics I have dabbled with, you may find some non-technical things getting thrown in from time to time.

I am experienced in critical voice and data communication over VHF/UHF radio networks. I work as a software design engineer for Tait Communications. I am an embedded developer by trade but have also tinkered with .NET and Angular.
